## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Core Voice Attributes

#### 1. **Direct & Clear**
- **What it means:** Say what you mean, no fluff or jargon
- **Why it matters:** Agents are busy; they need to understand value in 5 seconds
- **Example (Good):** "Replace 5 tools with one. Save $6,000/year."
- **Example (Bad):** "Leverage our integrated ecosystem to optimize your technology stack."

#### 2. **Results-Driven**
- **What it means:** Lead with outcomes, not features
- **Why it matters:** Agents care about closing deals, not technology
- **Example (Good):** "Close 35% more deals with AI-powered lead scoring"
- **Example (Bad):** "Our proprietary machine learning algorithms analyze contact data"

#### 3. **Real Estate Insider**
- **What it means:** Use industry language that agents use daily
- **Why it matters:** Builds trust, shows we understand their world
- **Example (Good):** "Track buyers, listings, and investment deals in one CRM"
- **Example (Bad):** "Manage all customer relationships across multiple verticals"

#### 4. **Data-Driven**
- **What it means:** Use specific numbers, not vague claims
- **Why it matters:** Agents are skeptical of marketing; data builds credibility
- **Example (Good):** "Save 15 hours/week on admin tasks"
- **Example (Bad):** "Dramatically reduce time spent on paperwork"

#### 5. **Approachable & Helpful**
- **What it means:** Friendly expert, not corporate vendor
- **Why it matters:** Real estate is a relationship business; tone matters
- **Example (Good):** "We built SAI for agents like you—tired of juggling 5+ tools"
- **Example (Bad):** "SAI™ delivers enterprise-grade solutions for real estate professionals"

### Tone Variations by Context

#### Website Homepage
- **Tone:** Confident, clear, benefit-focused
- **Style:** Short sentences, bullet points, bold stats
- **Example:** "One platform. Five core tools. $999/month. Replace your CRM, transaction manager, email marketing, market data, and AI assistant."

#### Product/Platform Page
- **Tone:** Educational, thorough, feature-rich
- **Style:** Longer descriptions, use cases, screenshots
- **Example:** "The Office handles 6 deal types—from home buying to FSBO—and auto-generates 8-15 processes per transaction. Never miss a deadline again."

#### Pricing Page
- **Tone:** Transparent, value-focused, ROI-driven
- **Style:** Clear tiers, feature comparisons, ROI calculator
- **Example:** "Most agents pay $629/month across 5 tools. SAI is $999/month for unlimited users. Add your whole team for free."

#### Success Stories/Testimonials
- **Tone:** Authentic, relatable, specific
- **Style:** First-person quotes, real metrics, before/after
- **Example:** "I was paying $850/month for 4 tools. SAI gave me more features for $999, and I added my assistant for free. Best decision I made." — Sarah T., Solo Agent

#### Blog/Educational Content
- **Tone:** Helpful, expert, non-salesy
- **Style:** How-to guides, best practices, industry insights
- **Example:** "How to Build a Buyer Pipeline in 30 Days (Without Cold Calling)"

#### Error Messages/UI Copy
- **Tone:** Helpful, clear, non-technical
- **Style:** Plain language, next steps, no blame
- **Example:** "We couldn't save your deal. Check your internet connection and try again."

#### Sales/Demo Scripts
- **Tone:** Consultative, curious, problem-solving
- **Style:** Questions first, features second, ROI close
- **Example:** "What tools are you using today? How much are you paying total? Let me show you how SAI replaces all of them for $999."

- **Claim:** "Save $6,000/year vs. buying tools separately"

- **Use case:** "I was paying $850/month. SAI is $999, but I added my assistant for free. Net savings: $7,200/year."

#### 2. **Time Savings** (45% of agents' pain point)
- **Claim:** "Save 15 hours/week on admin tasks"
- **Proof:** Agents spend 20 hrs/wk on admin (emails, data entry, scheduling). SAI automates 75% of this = 15 hrs saved.
- **Specifics:** Auto follow-ups (3 hrs), deal checklists (4 hrs), social scheduling (2 hrs), CRM entry (6 hrs)
- **Use case:** "I used to spend Sundays doing admin. Now SAI handles it, and I have weekends back."

#### 3. **Revenue Increase** (30% of agents' top goal)
- **Claim:** "Close 35% more deals with AI-powered lead scoring"
- **Proof:** Average agent closes 12 deals/year. With SAI's lead scoring, focus on HOT leads increases close rate from 8% to 11% = 4 extra deals/year = 35% increase.
- **Calculation:** 12 deals * 35% = 4 extra deals. At $10k commission/deal = $40k extra revenue.
- **Use case:** "I closed 18 deals last year. This year with SAI, I'm at 24 deals on pace."

#### 4. **All-in-One Simplicity** (25% of agents' frustration)
- **Claim:** "One login. One platform. No more juggling tools."
- **Proof:** Average agent uses 6.3 tools (CRM, TC, email, social, MLS, market data). Logging in/out wastes 30 min/day = 2.5 hrs/week.
- **Relief:** "No more switching tabs, copying data, or syncing contacts manually."
- **Use case:** "I had 7 tabs open every morning. Now it's just SAI. So much cleaner."

## Writing Guidelines

### Word Choice

#### Use Real Estate Language
- **Deal** (not "opportunity" or "pipeline item")
- **Closing** (not "completion" or "finalization")
- **Listing** (not "property posting")
- **Commission** (not "fee" or "payment")
- **MLS** (not "property database")
- **Buyer/Seller** (not "client" unless context is clear)
- **Transaction** (for formal contexts like "transaction management")
- **Lead** (for prospects, not "contact" until qualified)

#### Avoid Generic Business Jargon
- ❌ "Leverage our platform to optimize your workflow"
- ✅ "Use SAI to close more deals faster"
- ❌ "Transform your business with cutting-edge solutions"
- ✅ "Replace 5 tools with one platform"
- ❌ "Empower your team to achieve their full potential"
- ✅ "Give your team one login instead of five"

#### Use Specific Numbers (Not Vague Claims)
- ❌ "Save significant time on admin tasks"
- ✅ "Save 15 hours/week on admin tasks"
- ❌ "Dramatically reduce your tool costs"
- ✅ "Save $6,000/year vs. buying tools separately"
- ❌ "Close more deals with our platform"
- ✅ "Close 35% more deals with AI lead scoring"

#### Power Words for CTAs
- **Primary CTAs:** Start, Try, Get, Join, Calculate
- **Secondary CTAs:** Schedule, See, Learn, Compare, Explore
- **Urgency (use sparingly):** Today, Now, Limited, Exclusive

---

### Sentence Structure

#### Keep Sentences Short (10-15 words ideal)
- ❌ "Our platform provides real estate agents, teams, and brokerages with a comprehensive suite of tools designed to streamline operations and increase productivity."
- ✅ "SAI gives agents, teams, and brokerages 5 tools in one platform."

#### Use Active Voice
- ❌ "Leads are automatically scored by SAI's AI"
- ✅ "SAI's AI automatically scores your leads"
- ❌ "Deals can be tracked from start to finish"
- ✅ "Track deals from start to finish"

#### Lead with Benefits (Not Features)
- ❌ "SAI includes a transaction management module with 6 deal types"
- ✅ "Never miss a closing deadline with 6 deal-type workflows"
- ❌ "REID provides market data for 10+ markets"
- ✅ "Get institutional-grade market data without a $300/month subscription"

#### Use "You" Language (Not "We" or "I")
- ❌ "We built SAI to help agents close more deals"
- ✅ "Close more deals with SAI"
- ❌ "Our platform includes..."
- ✅ "You get CRM, transaction management, marketing..."

---

### Formatting Best Practices

#### Use Bullet Points for Lists (Not Paragraphs)
- ❌ "SAI includes CRM for lead management, The Office for transaction management, Content Studio for marketing automation, REID for market intelligence, and Global SAI for AI assistance."
- ✅:
  - CRM for lead management
  - The Office for transaction management
  - Content Studio for marketing automation
  - REID for market intelligence
  - Global SAI for AI assistance

#### Bold Key Metrics
- "Save **15 hours/week** on admin tasks"
- "Close **35% more deals** with AI lead scoring"
- "**$999/month** for unlimited users"

#### Use Headings to Break Up Content
- Don't write 500-word paragraphs
- Use H2 for major sections, H3 for subsections
- Every 100-150 words, add a heading or visual break

#### Use Visual Hierarchy
- **Headline (H1):** Largest, boldest (48-64px)
- **Subheadline:** 20-24px, lighter weight
- **Body:** 16-18px, readable font (Inter, system-ui)
- **CTA Button:** 16-18px, bold, high contrast

## Messaging Dos & Don'ts

### DO:
- ✅ Use real estate terminology (deals, closings, listings, commissions)
- ✅ Lead with benefits, not features ("Close more deals" not "AI lead scoring")
- ✅ Use specific numbers (35% more, 15 hours, $6,000)
- ✅ Keep sentences short (10-15 words)
- ✅ Use active voice ("SAI scores leads" not "Leads are scored by SAI")
- ✅ Address objections head-on (cost, switching, learning curve)
- ✅ Use testimonials and social proof
- ✅ Offer multiple CTAs (trial, demo, ROI calculator)
- ✅ Write for skimmers (headings, bullets, bold text)

### DON'T:
- ❌ Use generic business jargon ("leverage", "optimize", "transform")
- ❌ Oversell or hype ("revolutionary", "game-changing", "world's #1")
- ❌ Use vague claims ("save time", "close more deals" without numbers)
- ❌ Write long paragraphs (>3 sentences)
- ❌ Use passive voice ("Deals can be tracked...")
- ❌ Ignore objections (address them proactively)
- ❌ Use stock photos of fake agents (use real testimonials or abstract visuals)
- ❌ Overuse emojis (1-2 max, only in casual contexts)

## Testing & Validation

### Message Testing Framework

#### 1. **Clarity Test**
- **Question:** Can a 10-year-old understand this?
- **Pass:** "Replace 5 tools with one platform"
- **Fail:** "Leverage our integrated ecosystem to optimize your tech stack"

#### 2. **Relevance Test**
- **Question:** Does this speak to real estate agents specifically?
- **Pass:** "Track buyers, listings, and investment deals in one CRM"
- **Fail:** "Manage all customer relationships across multiple verticals"

#### 3. **Credibility Test**
- **Question:** Is this claim backed by data or proof?
- **Pass:** "Save 15 hours/week on admin tasks (based on agent surveys)"
- **Fail:** "Dramatically reduce time spent on paperwork"

#### 4. **Differentiation Test**
- **Question:** Could a competitor say this?
- **Pass:** "$999/month for unlimited users" (unique to SAI)
- **Fail:** "Easy to use CRM for real estate" (anyone can say this)

#### 5. **CTA Test**
- **Question:** Is the next step clear and low-friction?
- **Pass:** "Start Free Trial (no credit card required)"
- **Fail:** "Request Information" or "Contact Sales"

---

### A/B Testing Priorities

#### Homepage Hero (High Impact)
- **Test 1:** "Run Your Entire Real Estate Business from One Platform" vs. "Replace 5 Tools with One. Save $6,000/Year."
- **Test 2:** Primary CTA "Start Free Trial" vs. "Calculate Your Savings" (ROI calculator)
- **Test 3:** Trust signals (5,000+ agents) vs. Guarantee (14-day free trial, no CC)

- **Test 1:** Lead with FREE tier vs. Lead with ELITE tier

- **Test 1:** Module overview (5 cards) vs. Use cases (4 personas)
- **Test 2:** Demo video (2 min) vs. Interactive tour (click through)
- **Test 3:** Testimonials vs. Metrics (5,000+ agents, 50,000+ deals)
