## RISK MANAGEMENT

### Identified Risks & Mitigation

**Risk 1: Loss of SEO value from removed pages**
- **Impact:** High
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**
  - Set up 301 redirects for all old URLs
  - Keep URL structure similar where possible
  - Implement comprehensive internal linking
  - Submit new sitemap immediately
- **Owner:** Technical lead

**Risk 2: Lack of real customer testimonials**
- **Impact:** Medium
- **Probability:** High (if in early/beta stage)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Use beta tester feedback initially
  - Create template success stories with "Based on beta results" disclaimers
  - Prioritize collecting real testimonials ASAP
  - Offer incentives for testimonials (discount, feature in marketing)
- **Owner:** Marketing lead

- **Impact:** High
- **Probability:** Low (if done correctly)
- **Mitigation:**

- **Owner:** Project manager

**Risk 4: Technical bugs post-launch**
- **Impact:** Medium
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### About the Transformation

**Q: Why are we pivoting from AI consulting to real estate SaaS?**
A: SAI platform has been developed as the core product. The consulting business model doesn't match the SaaS product we've built. Real estate agents are our target market, and we need a site that speaks directly to them.

**Q: What happens to the AI consulting business?**
A: This transformation focuses the website entirely on SAI platform. If consulting services continue, they'd need a separate site or subdomain.

**Q: Will we lose SEO value?**
A: Some loss is inevitable, but we're mitigating with 301 redirects, maintaining URL structure where possible, and comprehensive internal linking. Long-term, real estate-focused content will rank better for our target keywords.

**Q: How long will the transformation take?**
A: Full transformation: 6-8 weeks. Homepage priority: 2-3 weeks. We can launch phases gradually.

### About Implementation

**Q: Can we do this in phases or must it be all at once?**
A: Recommended phased approach: Homepage â†’ Core Pages â†’ Content â†’ Polish. This lets us see results faster and iterate.

**Q: What if we don't have real customer testimonials yet?**
A: Use success story templates based on beta results with appropriate disclaimers. Prioritize collecting real testimonials immediately after launch.

**Q: Do we need to create all content before launch?**
A: No. Launch with core pages (Homepage, Platform, Pricing) and 5-10 content pieces. Content creation is ongoing.

**Q: How do we handle the existing blog posts?**
A: Replace gradually. Redirect old posts to related new content. Create 10 real estate posts for launch, then 2-4 per month ongoing.

### About SAI Platform

**Q: What if features aren't built yet?**
A: Be transparent. Clearly mark "Coming Q1 2025" or "Coming 2026" on roadmap. Focus marketing on available features now.

**Q: How do we explain pricing?**
A: Three simple tiers: Free (trial), Elite ($999/mo), Custom (enterprise). Be transparent. Highlight unlimited users as differentiator.

**Q: What's our main competitive advantage?**
A: Four-fold: (1) All-in-one (vs. multiple tools), (2) Real estate-specialized (vs. generic CRM), (3) Unlimited users (vs. per-agent pricing), (4) AI-native (vs. AI bolted on).
