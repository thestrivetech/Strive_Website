## ASPIRATIONAL (Don't Claim At All)

Do NOT make these claims without verified data:

### Fabricated Statistics (REMOVE)
- "391% increase in conversions" - No verified source
- "119% higher click-through rates" - No verified source
- "3.7x ROI within 90 days" - Completely fabricated
- "90% user adoption in 30 days" - Fabricated metric
- "95% reduction in compliance issues" - No data
- "85-92% AI accuracy" - Unverified claim

### Unverifiable Performance Claims
- Specific percentage improvements without data
- Specific time savings without user studies
- Specific ROI numbers without case studies
- Specific deal closure improvements without tracking

### Fake Case Studies (REMOVE)
- MedPractice healthcare case study - Fabricated
- Regional Bank case study - Fabricated
- IndustrialTech manufacturing case study - Fabricated
- Any fake agent personas (Sarah, Marcus, David) - Replace with Strive Tech Team

## ATTRIBUTION REQUIREMENTS

When using industry statistics, include:
1. Source name (e.g., "NAR", "McKinsey")
2. Year of publication
3. Ideally: direct URL in data files

Example format for data files:
```typescript
{
  stat: "68%",
  context: "of agents have adopted AI",
  source: "NAR 2025 Technology Survey",
  sourceUrl: "https://nar.realtor/research-and-statistics/research-reports/realtor-technology-survey",
  year: 2025
}
```
